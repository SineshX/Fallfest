Stomach Related:
Symptoms:

Type1:
1)Loose or watery stools that persist for weeks
2)Abdominal cramps,Bloating.
3)Nausea.

Type2:
1)Abdominal cramps and pain.
2)Nausea, vomiting or both.
3)Occasional muscle aches or headache.
4)Low-grade fever.

Type3:
1)Vomiting or vomiting blood — which may appear red or black.
2)Dark blood in stools, or stools that are black or tarry.
3)Trouble breathing,Feeling faint.
4)Nausea or vomiting.
5)Unexplained weight loss.
